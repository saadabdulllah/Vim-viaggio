# Vim-viaggio

## Cursor movement word by word
#### cursor at End of the line: `$`
#### cursor at Beginning of the line: `0`
#### Next Word [cursor at Beginning]: `w`
#### Next Word [cursor at end]: `e`
#### Previous Word [cursor at Beginning]: `e`

## Arrow
#### LEFT : `h`
#### RIGHT: `l`
#### UP: `k`
#### DOWN: `j`

## Deleting 
#### delete whole line: `dd`
